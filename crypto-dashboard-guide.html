Crypto Dashboard Guide

A dedicated dashboard helps you keep tabs on market prices and news in one place. This guide walks you through setting up a basic portfolio tracker.

Why Use a Dashboard?

  • Monitor your holdings across multiple exchanges
  • Receive alerts on key price movements
  • Visualize performance with charts and analytics

Getting Started

Choose a secure tracking tool or build a spreadsheet to import balances from Gemini, Coinbase, or any wallet. Enable two-factor authentication on every connected account.
