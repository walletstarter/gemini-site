Gemini Fee Schedule (2025)

Gemini offers transparent pricing for every trade. Using the ActiveTrader interface dramatically reduces costs compared to the standard web version.

Trading Fees

  • Standard: up to 1.49% per trade
  • ActiveTrader: maker 0.40% / taker 0.20% and lower with volume

Withdrawal Costs

  • Free ACH withdrawals
  • Crypto withdrawal fees vary by asset (about 0.001 BTC for Bitcoin)

How to Minimize Fees

Enable ActiveTrader and fund via bank transfer to keep your trading costs at a minimum. Large traders can qualify for further discounts.
